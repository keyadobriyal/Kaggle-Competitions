# Kaggle Playground Series Challenge - December 2025
## Diabetes Prediction Challenge
### Playground Series - Season 5, Episode 12

 **Goal:** Predict the probability that a patient will be diagnosed with diabetes.

**Evaluation**

Submissions are evaluated on area under the ROC curve between the predicted probability and the observed target.

The winter vacation of December I decided to start with the playground series challenge along with the IBM Machine Learning Professional Certificate almost under completion, this was one of the opportunity to apply gained knowledge in real playground.

I could attempt two submissions, first using Machine Learning Methodology using Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost and LightGBM to predict the probability that a patient will be diagnosed with diabetes achieving an ROC	0.694757 using Logistic Regression in the first atttempt.

In my second attempt, I used Machine Learning Pipeline using above four architecture with decision threshold and cost threshold optimization with best score of ROC 0.69221.

The competition ended me being at 2330 Rank out of 4208 participants on the leaderboard, a satisfying effort.
